What DevOps Is:

  • Culture & Operational Model
  • Focuses on Fast Feedback Cycles
  • Sustainability

What DevOps Is Not:

  1. A Group
  2. A Person
  3. A Set of Tools
  4. Puppet
  5. A Workload

Core Values of DevOps:

  1. Individuals and Interaction OVER Processes and Tools
  2. Working Software OVER Comprehensive Documentation
  3. Customer Collaboration OVER Contract Negotiation
  4. Responding to Change OVER Following a plan

Advantages of DevOps:

  1. 200x Faster Time To Market
  2. 2x Goal Achievement Rate
  3. 60x Fewer Failures
